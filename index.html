BIOTECHNOLOGY

Biotechnology uses living organisms, cells, and biological processes to create products and technologies, merging biology with engineering and other sciences like chemistry and genetics.

Core concepts

  • Interdisciplinary: Combines natural sciences (biology, genetics, microbiology) with engineering and technology.
  • Application-focused: Aims to solve problems and create useful products, improving human life.
  • Uses biological systems: Leverages cells, organisms, or their components (like DNA).
