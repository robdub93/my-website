key areas and examples

  1. Medicine/Healthcare: Developing vaccines, therapeutic proteins (like insulin), gene therapies, and diagnostics.
  2. Agriculture: Creating genetically modified (GM) crops for pest resistance or higher yield, improving food security.
  3. Industrial Biotech: Producing chemicals, textiles, biofuels, and using enzymes in manufacturing.
  4. Environmental Biotech: Bio-remediation (cleaning pollution) and developing sustainable processes.
